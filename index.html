About Us

Fanays Travel & Cargo is a Nairobi-based travel and logistics agency built on trust, convenience, and exceptional service. From air ticketing to cargo solutions, we bring the world closer by providing seamless travel experiences and dependable transport management. Our commitment is to make every journey — whether personal or business — smooth, safe, and unforgettable.
